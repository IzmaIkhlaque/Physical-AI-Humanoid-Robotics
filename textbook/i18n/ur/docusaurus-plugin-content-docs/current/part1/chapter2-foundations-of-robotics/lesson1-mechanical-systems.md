# Mechanical Systems in Robotics

## Learning Objectives
- Understand structural components of robots
- Learn about materials and manufacturing
- Grasp mechanical design principles

## Robot Structure
Robots consist of links connected by joints forming kinematic chains.

## Materials
- Aluminum: Lightweight, common
- Carbon fiber: High strength-to-weight
- Steel: Heavy-duty applications
