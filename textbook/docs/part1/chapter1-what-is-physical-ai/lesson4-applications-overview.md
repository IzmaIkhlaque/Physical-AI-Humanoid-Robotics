# Applications of Physical AI

## Industrial Applications
- Manufacturing: Welding, painting, assembly
- Warehouse: Amazon operates 750,000+ robots
- Logistics: Autonomous vehicles

## Service Robotics
- Healthcare: Surgical robots, rehabilitation
- Hospitality: Hotel and restaurant robots

## Consumer Robotics
- Vacuum robots: 40M+ Roombas sold
- Lawn robots, educational robots

## Emerging: Humanoid Robots
Tesla Optimus, Figure 01, and others pursuing general-purpose humanoids.

## Key Takeaways
:::tip Summary
- Industrial robotics is mature ($50B+ market)
- Humanoid robots are the next frontier
:::
